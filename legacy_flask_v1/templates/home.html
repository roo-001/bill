Features

Everything you need for shared living.

👥

Member Management

Add or remove household members in seconds. Everyone gets a clear view of who's part of the pool.

📋

Expense Recording

Log any shared expense with a name and amount. BillBreak stores and organizes every entry automatically.

⚡

Auto-Split

Bills are instantly divided equally among all members. No manual math, no arguments about who owes what.

📊

Central Dashboard

A single view showing total expenses, per-person share, and every recorded bill — always up to date.

🔒

Transparent History

Every expense is logged with who added it and when. Full accountability, zero confusion.

🛠️

Lightweight & Fast

Built on Flask and SQLite — no bloat, no subscriptions. Just a simple tool that works.
